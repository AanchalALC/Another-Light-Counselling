Why Choose This Path?

Not everyone can afford Aanchal’s standard rates. This onboarding plan bridges that gap—start with her direct care, then continue long-term with a mapped Another Light Counselling therapist plus her behind-the-scenes supervision.

  • Therapy continues with your mapped Another Light Counselling therapist at standard affordable rates.
  • Aanchal will not directly join your sessions post-handover.
  • Supervision remains internal between Aanchal and your therapist.
  • You can request updates anytime from your therapist or our manager.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this plan about?

It’s a structured way to begin therapy with Aanchal’s direct care and continue with an ALC therapist under her ongoing supervision.

How does it work?

You start with 3 sessions with Aanchal, get mapped to a suitable Another Light Counselling therapist, and continue therapy with her behind-the-scenes guidance.

Why choose this plan?

It bridges expert care and affordability — you receive Aanchal’s insight without her full-session rates.

What happens after my 3 sessions?

You continue therapy with your mapped Another Light Counselling therapist. Aanchal remains involved through internal supervision.

Will Aanchal attend my sessions after handover?

No, but she supervises your therapist to ensure ongoing quality and continuity of care.

Can I extend her supervision later?

Yes, you can continue Aanchal’s supervision for another 3 months at a nominal add-on cost.

What are the plan options?

Individual (₹10,000) and Couple (₹12,000) onboarding packages, plus optional EMDR, IFS, and combo plans.

What is supervision and how does it help me?

It’s a process where Aanchal guides your therapist — so your sessions benefit from her expertise even indirectly.

Can I return to Aanchal later?

Yes. You can book EMDR or IFS sessions directly with her once onboarding is complete.

How do I sign up?

Contact Team Another Light Counselling via email or WhatsApp to discuss which plan fits your goals best.
